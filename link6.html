Space has time. At the same time, it has no time.
Granting time to space is generally another object's time.
If there are no objects to give time to space,
past present and future appear simultaneously.
In this case, It is difficult to feel time.

Make a time-space model by applying movement at each time of this space.
The time-space model exists by movement.
There is no time. Only, There is movement.
In this model, you can feel the interactions
between past present and future time with timeless.
