ecgData/ art/ interactive

As technology develops, we become increasingly objectified and processed into data.
Humans will be fully processed with data as soon as possible.
We investigate some of the human being extracted with data.
They study the new meanings they have to be transmitted and how they can exist in the system.

In this work, human ECG (electrocardiogram) data is extracted in real time.
The spectator will face his organs extracted from his / her data in real time.
